the project

touch the maps in your brain is a collection of poetry written for the senior English seminar class at BYU-Hawaiʻi.

my thesis

"Through poetry, I set out to explore the transformation of multi-dimensional space and sense in poetic expression within technological innovations of the social and scientific spheres, with a special focus on the connections to mental and emotional experiences."
